Recipe #2

  • Time: 10 Minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Pre-Ordered Sugar Free Icecream 1 1/2 Cup
Protein Powder 1 Scoop (33g)

Step 1: Add Follwing Ingredients to a bowl:
- 1 1/2 Cup Sugar Free Icecream
- 1/2 Scoop (16g) Protein Powder
Step 2: Mix Together
Step 3: Add Another 1/2 Scoop (16g) Protein Powder
Step 4: Mix Again Until Completely Together

Recipe 3

  • Time: 45 Minutes
  • Serves: 12
Ingredients Quantity
Bananas 3
Protein Powder 2 Scoop (66g)
Peanut Butter 1 Cup
Cocoa Powder 1/4 Cup

Step 1: Blend Together:
- 3 Bananas
- 2 Scoops Protein Powder
- 1/4 Cup Cocoa Powder
Step 2: Melt 1 Cup Peanut Butter In the Microwave Until Liquid
Step 3: Add Melted Peanut Butter Into Blender
Step 4: Blend All Ingredients
Step 5: Pour Into Baking tray
Step 6: Bake At 350 Farenheit for 20 Mnutes
Step 7: Let Cool Down And Enjoy :)
